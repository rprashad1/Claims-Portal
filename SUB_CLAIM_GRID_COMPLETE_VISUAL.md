### Button States

```
Page 1 of 5:
[First] [<<] [<] - DISABLED (already on first page)
1 of 5 - ACTIVE
[>] [>>] [Last] - ENABLED (more pages available)

[First] [<<] [<] - ENABLED (can go back)
3 of 5 - ACTIVE

[First] [<<] [<] - ENABLED (can go back)

[>] [>>] [Last] - DISABLED (already on last page)
```

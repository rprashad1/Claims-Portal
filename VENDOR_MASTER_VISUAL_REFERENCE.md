## ?? Workflow Diagrams

### Add New Vendor Workflow
```
Click [Add New Vendor]
    ?
Modal Opens (Blank Form)
    ?
Fill Basic Info
    ?
Add At Least 1 Main Address
    ?
Fill Contact Info
    ?
Configure Payment (Optional)
    ?
Click [Save Vendor]
    ?
Validation
    ?? Pass ? ? Vendor Created
    ?? Fail ? ? Show Errors
    ?
Modal Closes
    ?
Return to Results
```

```
Enter Search Term
    ?

    ?

    ?

    ?

    ?
Modal Opens with Vendor Data
    ?
Make Changes
    ?
Click [Save Vendor]
    ?
Validation
    ?? Pass ? ? Vendor Updated
    ?? Fail ? ? Show Errors
    ?
Modal Closes
    ?
Results Refresh
```

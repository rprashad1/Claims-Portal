## User Interaction Flow (Updated)

```
USER JOURNEY: Creating Driver and Feature

?? Step 1: Select Driver Type
?  ?? Choose: Insured / Listed / Unlisted
?  ?? Form updates based on selection
?
?? Step 2: Fill Driver Information
?  ?? If unlisted:
?  ?  ?? Fill Name (required)
?  ?  ?? Fill License # (optional)
?  ?  ?? Fill License State (optional)
?  ?  ?? See Date of Birth (defaults to today)
?  ?
?  ?? If insured/listed:
?     ?? See info message about policy records
?
?? Step 3: Select Injury Status
?  ?? Choose: Yes / No
?  ?
?  ?? If YES:
?     ?? Fill injury details
?     ?? Fill attorney info (if needed)
?     ?? Button enables: "Save Driver & Create Feature"
?
?? Step 4: Create Feature
?  ?? Click: "Save Driver & Create Feature"
?  ?? Modal opens: Feature/Sub-Claim Form
?  ?
?  ?? Fill:
?  ?  ?? Coverage type
?  ?  ?? Coverage limits
?  ?  ?? Reserves
?  ?  ?? Assigned adjuster
?  ?
?  ?? Click: "Save & Create Feature"
?
?? Step 5: Feature Created
?  ?? Feature added to grid
?  ?? DriverSaved = true
?  ?? Driver form cleared
?  ?? ? NextButton = ENABLED
?
?? Step 6: Proceed to Next Step
   ?? Click: "Next ?"
      ?? Go to Step 4: Third Parties
```

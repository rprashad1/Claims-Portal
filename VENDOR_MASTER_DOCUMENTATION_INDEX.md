## FAQ

**Q: Where is the Vendor Master module?**
A: Navigate to Administration ? Vendor Master

**Q: How do I add a vendor?**
A: See `VENDOR_MASTER_QUICK_START.md` ? Step 2

**Q: What are the validation rules?**
A: See `VENDOR_MASTER_IMPLEMENTATION_GUIDE.md` ? Validation Rules

**Q: How do I search vendors?**
A: See `VENDOR_MASTER_QUICK_START.md` ? Search Operators

**Q: Can I delete vendors?**
A: No, only disable/enable (soft delete). See Business Rules.

**Q: Can I have multiple main addresses?**
A: No, exactly 1 main address per vendor. See Business Rules.

**Q: What payment options are available?**
A: Monthly (dates 1-31) or Weekly (Mon-Fri). See Payment section.

**Q: How do I configure payment frequency?**
A: See `VENDOR_MASTER_VISUAL_REFERENCE.md` ? Payment Configuration Flows

**Q: What's the difference between Individual and Business?**
A: Business entities require DBA field. See Entity Type Behavior.

**Q: Can I disable vendors?**
A: Yes, click Disable button. Vendors can be re-enabled. See Soft Delete.
